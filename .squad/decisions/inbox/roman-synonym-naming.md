# Decision: Synonym Translation Naming Round

**Author:** Roman (Market Researcher)  
**Date:** 2026-07-18  
**Status:** Proposed

## Context

Fourth naming round — translated 14 English concepts across Greek, Latin, and Sanskrit. Yashas's current favorites: Ocella (#1), Restwell (#2).

## Findings

- **Best new candidate: Anesis** (Greek, "relief/relaxation") — elegant, 3 syllables, completely clean App Store, captures what the app delivers
- **Best new Sanskrit: Nimesha** ("blink/moment") — poetic double meaning, clean, strong yin-yang resonance
- **Best new Latin: Mollis** ("soft/tender") — sounds like what it means, 2 syllables, clean
- "Gentle/soft" concept words are the richest naming vein across all three languages
- Watch/guard words are heavily contested on App Store — avoid this semantic space

## Recommendation

Yashas's instinct on Ocella and Restwell remains well-placed. From this round, Anesis, Nimesha, and Mollis deserve shortlist consideration alongside those two. Full report in `docs/app-naming-synonyms.md`.
