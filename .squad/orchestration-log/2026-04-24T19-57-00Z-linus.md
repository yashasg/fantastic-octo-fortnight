### Design Pattern

- **iOS HIG Compliance:** Settings screens presented modally get a Done/Close button; pushed screens get a back button. Since SettingsView has its own internal NavigationStack, Done is the correct affordance.

- **Environment Injection:** Sheet re-injects EnvironmentObjects because sheet environment inheritance is version-dependent (iOS 16+).
